About Me

I'm a passionate researcher currently pursuing my BSc in Bioinformatics at the University of Macau, where I maintain a strong academic record with a cumulative GPA of 3.49/4.0 and ranked in the top 15% of my class.

My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical applications, with particular expertise in deep learning, computer vision, and multimodal data fusion. I'm passionate about collaborating on medical image processing, image segmentation, classification, object detection, medical robots, and trajectory registration for autonomous driving. I've had the privilege of conducting research across diverse domains and have contributed to multiple publications in high-impact venues.

Beyond academics, I enjoy photography, travel, and sports. I'm always eager to explore new technologies and apply AI solutions to real-world biomedical challenges, from quantitative finance sentiment analysis to cutting-edge medical imaging applications.
